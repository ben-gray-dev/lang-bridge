Our Mission
LangBridge seeks to promote bridge-building between linguistic communities by empowering language learners of all backgrounds especially those from vulnerable, linguistically-isolated populations in the United States.

Machine Learning
The Pareto Principle states that 20% of the effort equates to 80% of the results. By teaching the most frequent words, we maximize percent recognition of new speech. For example, by learning the first 1000 most common words in the English language, one can understand 80% of spoken English!
Several studies have confirmed that memory retention is optimized by increasingly long intervals for repetition of concepts. Given the ease with which you answer each prompt, we determine when that term be reviewed in a different context.
Based upon your learning habits, we use machine learning to adapt a personalized study schedule which maximizes recall.
